Penrose Tiles

Penrose Tiles is a Phi & Form app for creating rich visual compositions from aperiodic tiles. It turns a mathematically rigorous system into something tactile, exploratory, and aesthetically rewarding.

What it offers

Compose, adjust, and study aperiodic structure.

Use it as a creative tool first, then discover the logic that makes the pattern possible.

Features

Advanced tools that shape how Penrose compositions evolve.

Aperiodic Build Mode

Compose directly with two Penrose tile types while keeping the non-repeating structure coherent as the design grows.

Local-To-Global Variation

Small tile changes propagate through the pattern, making it easy to test variations and discover strong visual outcomes.

Balance Controls

Refine contrast, density, and pattern rhythm to tune both the mathematical clarity and final visual expression.

Pattern Study View

Move between artistic composition and structural reading so the geometry stays understandable while you create.
