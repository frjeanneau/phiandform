Penrose Tiles

Penrose Tiles is a Phi & Form app for creating rich visual compositions from aperiodic tiles. It turns a mathematically rigorous system into something tactile, exploratory, and aesthetically rewarding.

Features

Pattern Mode

Complete a central rosette and the pattern expands automatically, repeating your tiles around the center to build larger symmetric structures.

Color

Long press a tile to open the color picker and build palettes that clarify structure and emphasize rhythm.

Export

Export clean screenshots, 15‑second replay movies, and saved designs to revisit later.

Browse

Open the Library for saved designs or the Gallery for built‑in compositions ready to explore.
