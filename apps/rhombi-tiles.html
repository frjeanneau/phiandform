Rhombi Tiles

Rhombi Tiles is a Phi & Form app for building visual compositions from rhombus-based systems. It turns repetition, symmetry, and local rule into something tactile, playful, and immediate. By arranging rhombi directly, you can discover how symmetry, adjacency, and proportion create visual coherence.

Pattern Mode

Color

Apply palettes to highlight symmetry, rhythm, and contrast as the tiling grows.

Export

Export screenshots, short replay movies, and saved designs to revisit later.

Browse
