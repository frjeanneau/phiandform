What it offers

Compose with repetition, symmetry, and variation.

Rhombi Tiles makes geometric structure something you can arrange directly, then understand through the patterns that emerge.

Overview

A study in rhombus tilings and visual order.

Rhombi Tiles focuses on the expressive power of simple quadrilateral rules. By arranging rhombi directly, users can discover how symmetry, adjacency, and proportion produce visual coherence.

Core ideas

Rhombus tilings

Rhombi create a versatile family of tilings because their angles and edge lengths can lock together in multiple ways while still preserving local consistency across the surface.

Core ideas

Symmetry and variation

Repetition, rotation, and reflection provide the grammar of the system, while small changes in arrangement create a wide range of visual outcomes from the same underlying rules.
