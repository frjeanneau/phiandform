Mathematics

The site should explain the ideas without forcing all the depth onto the homepage.

01

Keep the homepage concise.

The landing page should introduce the mathematical lens, not become a textbook. Its job is to orient, intrigue, and guide.

02

Give each app its own mathematical narrative.

Penrose tilings, tessellations, symmetry groups, proportion, or transformations can each live on the app page where they matter.

03

Split into deeper pages only when needed.

If an app eventually needs references, diagrams, or long-form writing, that can become a deeper linked page later.

About

ϕ & Form explores the space between mathematics and visual art.

ϕ & Form is a design studio built from a simple idea: mathematics is not only logical, it is beautiful.

At the intersection of geometry, proportion, and interaction, the studio designs digital experiences that reveal the elegance of structure and turn mathematical principles into intuitive visual play.

Through carefully crafted apps and interactive tools, ϕ & Form invites people to experience mathematics as movement, pattern, and design.

Growth model

  • Homepage for the studio and featured apps
  • One detail page per app
  • Optional deeper mathematics pages later
