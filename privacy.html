Privacy

Privacy policy for Phi & Form.

This policy explains how Phi & Form LLC handles information in connection with phiandform.com and Phi & Form apps, including Penrose Tiles and Rhombi Tiles. Last updated March 4, 2026.

How data is handled Contact us
At a glance Phi & Form

Current approach

Minimal collection. No advertising. No sale of personal data.

Phi & Form apps are designed to work without user accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud-sync features. We do not sell personal information and we do not use in-app tracking for targeted advertising.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, requests for deletion, or concerns about data handling, contact us at hello@phiandform.com.

Overview

What this policy covers.

This policy applies to the Phi & Form website, publicly accessible pages operated by Phi & Form LLC, and Phi & Form applications made available through Apple platforms.

It is intended to describe what information we collect, how we use it, when it may be shared, how long it is kept, and how you can contact us about privacy-related requests.

Core principles

Privacy by design

  • No user accounts are required to use current Phi & Form apps.
  • No analytics SDKs or crash-reporting SDKs are used in current Phi & Form apps.
  • No cloud sync, contact upload, or account profile features are used in current Phi & Form apps.
  • No personal data is sold.
  • No tracking is used for targeted advertising.
  • We aim to collect only what is needed to operate, secure, and improve our work.

Data

What information we collect.

Information you provide

Direct communications

If you contact us by email or another direct channel, we may receive your name, email address, and any information you choose to include in your message.

Technical information

Website technical data

When you access phiandform.com, standard web-server records may include limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring page, and timestamps.

We use this information to operate the website, maintain security, and investigate technical problems or misuse.

What we do not collect

No unnecessary permissions

As of the date of this policy, Phi & Form apps do not collect contacts, calendars, photos, precise location, health information, payment card information, account credentials, or cloud-synced profile data.

No embedded tracking

No analytics or third-party advertising SDKs

Phi & Form apps do not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting SDKs as of the date of this policy.

If you download apps through Apple services, Apple may collect information under Apple’s own privacy terms. Apple’s handling of that information is governed by Apple’s policies, not this site policy.

Sharing

How information is used and when it may be shared.

Use

Why we use information

  • To operate and maintain the website and apps
  • To respond to support or privacy inquiries
  • To troubleshoot bugs and improve performance
  • To protect the service and comply with legal obligations

Sharing

When we may disclose information

We may share limited information with website hosting providers, Apple platform services, or professional advisors when reasonably necessary to operate the service, protect rights and security, or comply with law.

We do not sell personal information and we do not share personal information for targeted advertising.

Rights

Retention, deletion, and your choices.

Retention

How long information is kept

We keep direct communications only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to requests, maintain the website, address technical or security issues, and meet legal obligations.

Deletion and access

Your privacy choices

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have provided directly to us. If we are able to verify your request and are legally permitted to do so, we will respond within a reasonable time.

If you no longer want us to retain an email inquiry or support message, contact us and we will review the request.

Children

Children’s privacy

Phi & Form services are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and address the issue.
